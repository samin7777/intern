Technology

Technology, the application of scientific knowledge to the practical aims of human life or, as it is sometimes phrased, to the change and manipulation of the human environment.

Internet and Mobile Technology Has Improved Home and Work-Life. Automation Has Improved Manufacturing Practices.There Is a Greater Wealth of Information Available. The Entertainment Industry Has Grown. Technology Is a Time-Saver. Devices and Machines Function More Efficiently.



There are 6 mainly sixcategories of technology. They are:
• Construction
The design and development of new buildings and structures.
• Transportation
The movement of people or goods.Many methods: cars, trucks, airplane, trains,pipelines.
• Energy/Power
The power/fuel of technology. Considered the “food” of technology. All other areas depend on it.
• Communication
The sending and receiving of messages.
• Manufacturing
The design and development of products.
• Bio-Related
Technology related to life. This includes all technologies used in the medical field.

Conclusion

Thus, technology have both advantages as well as disadvantages. So we should know how to handle technology benificially.
